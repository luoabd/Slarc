Admission

An extraordinary freedom of opportunity—to explore, to
collaborate and to challenge yourself

Explore the possibilities of a SCU education as you map out your college journey.

We look for distinctive students who exhibit an abundance of energy and curiosity in their classes, activities, projects, research and lives.

SCU meets the full financial need of every admitted undergrad who qualifies for assistance.

Nearly 70% of undergrads receive financial aid. Generally, tuition is covered for families with incomes below RMB 125,000.
